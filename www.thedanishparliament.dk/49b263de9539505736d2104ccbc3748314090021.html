Simon Emil Ammitzbøll, born October 20th 1977 in Hillerød, son of old age pensioner Nils Ammitzbøll Henriksen and former social worker Annie Henriksen. Married to late Henning Olsen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for Liberal Alliance in Copenhagen greater constituency from September 15th 2011.
Member of Parliament for Liberal Alliance in Zealand greater constituency from June 16th 2009 to September 15th 2011.
Member of Parliament for Outside the parliamentary groups in Zealand greater constituency from October 8th 2008 to June 15th 2009.
Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in Zealand greater constituency from November 13th 2007 to October 7th 2008.
Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in Storstrøm County constituency from February 8th 2005 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for Liberal Alliance in all nomination districts in the constituency from 2009.
Candidate for the Social Liberal Party in Vordingborg nomination district from 2004 to 2006.
Candidate for the Social Liberal Party in Næstved nomination district from 2003 to 2008.

Parliamentary career
Minister for Economic Affairs and the Interior from November 28th 2016.
Member of the Public Accounts Committee from October 1st 2016 to November 28th 2016.

Spokesman on political affairs, constitutional matters and legal affairs 2009-2016, immigration and integration 2015-2016, health 2013-2015, immigration and integration affairs 2012-2013, cultural affairs and sports 2009-2013 and gender equality  2009-2012. Chairman of the Gender Equality Committee 2011-2012. Chairman of the parliamentary group of Liberal Alliance from 2010.


Education and jobs
BA (social science), Roskilde University Centre, from 2000 to 2003.
Sojourn, Krogerup High School, 1999.
Matriculated, Marie Kruses School, from 1993 to 1996.
Basic school, Marie Kruses School, from 1983 to 1993.

Editor of »Radikal Politik« (Social Liberal policy), from 2004 to 2005.


Affiliations
Member of the governing body of Krogerup High School from 1999, chairman from 2007. National chairman of the Young Social Liberal Party 2000-2003. Member of the board of governors of the Danish Youth Council (DUF) 2003-2005. Chairman of 'Kronprinsesse Ingrids Fond' from 2007.


Publications
Editor of the anniversary book »Tidens Tanker« (Contemporary Thoughts), 2001, and editor with Erik Boel of the anthology »Europa i alle palettens farver« (Europe in all the colours of the palette), 2007.


Awards
Received the Politician of the Year award from the Danish National Association of Gays & Lesbians (LBL) in 2006. Knight of the Order of Dannebrog in 2015.
