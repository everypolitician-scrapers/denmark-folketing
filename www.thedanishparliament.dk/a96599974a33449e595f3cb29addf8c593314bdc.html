Søren Espersen

The Danish People's Party,DF

Member of the Danish Parliament
The Danish People's Party

Former Member of the Presidium of the Danish Parliament
Folketinget, Christiansborg, 1240 Copenhagen K
Phone in the Parliament: +45 3337 5124
Email: dfsesp@ft.dk
Homepage: http://www.sorenespersen.dk

Søren Espersen, born July 20th 1953 in Svenstrup, Himmerland, son of Peder Christian Espersen and Inger Espersen, née Lund. Married to Yvette Kim Fay Espersen. The couple has five children, Ditte, Anders, Robin, Susie and Peter, and two grandchildren, Daniel, Mai and Josefine.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in Zealand greater constituency from June 18th 2015.
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in Greater Copenhagen greater constituency from November 13th 2007 to June 18th 2015.
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in Copenhagen County constituency from February 8th 2005 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Kalundborg nomination district from 2013.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Ballerup nomination district from 2007 to 2013.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Hvidovre nomination district from 2004 to 2006.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Sundby nomination district from 1997 to 2004.
Candidate for The Progress Party in Præstø nomination district from 1992 to 1995.

Parliamentary career
Member of the Presidium from November 28th 2007 to October 2nd 2012.

Spokesman on foreign affairs, Greenland and constitutional matters. Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee. Member of the Foreign Affairs Committee, the Defence Committee, the Standing Orders Committee, the Faroe Islands Committee, the Greenland Committee, the Cultural Affairs Committee and the Committee for Contact to the German Minority.  Member of the Defence Commission 2007-2009. Member of the Greenlandic-Danish Autonomy Commission 2005-2008. Member of the parliamentary assembly of the Organisation on Security and Cooperation in Europe (OSCE) 2005-2006. Member of the Nordic Council 2005-2006. Member of the parliamentary group board of the Danish People's Party, secretary of the parliamentary group from 2006. Vice-chairman of the Danish People's Party from 2012.


Education and jobs
Journalist examination, the Danish School of Journalism, from 1976 to 1980.
Studied theology, University og Copenhagen, from 1974 to 1976.
National service, Danish navy, from 1973 to 1974.
Matriculated, Hasseris Upper Secondary School, from 1970 to 1973.
Lower secondary school leaving certificate, Svenstrup School, from 1967 to 1970.

Head of public relations, the Danish People's Party, from 1997 to 2005.
Journalist, Billed-Bladet, from 1992 to 1996.
Freelance journalist, London, from 1989 to 1992.
Journalist, 'BT', from 1980 to 1988.
Journalist, 'Dagbladet', Ringsted, 1980.


Affiliations
Chairman of the Danish People's Party in the metropolitan region 1997-2000. Member of the management committee of 'Danmarks Radio' (Danish Broadcasting Corporation) 2001-2005. Chairman of the Danish People's Party's committee on election and strategy from 2002. Vice-chairman of the Raoul Wallenberg Association from 2003. Member of the central board of the Danish People's Party from 2006, political vice-chairman from 2012.


Publications
Author of »Danmarks fremtid – dit land, dit valg« (The Future of Denmark – Your Country, Your Choice), 2001, in collaboration with U. Dahlerup and A. Skjødt. »Valdemar Rørdam – Nationalskjald og Landsforræder« (Valdemar Rørdam – National Bard and Traitor), 2003, »Israels selvstændighedskrig - og de danske selvstændige« (The Israeli War of Independence - and the Danish Volunteers), 2007. Editor responsible under the press law of »Dansk Folkeblad« 1997-2005.
