Kim Christiansen, born September 14th 1956 in Slagelse, son of bricklayer Evald Christiansen and assistant nurse Birde Christiansen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in East Jutland greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in Århus County constituency from February 8th 2005 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Djurs nomination district from 2010.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Skanderborg nomination district from 2007 to 2010.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Grenaa nomination district from 2004 to 2006.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Hammel nomination district from 1999 to 2004.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on traffic. Member of the Transport and Building Committee, Business, Growth and Export Committee, the Fiscal Affairs Committee and the Board of the Danish Inter-Parliamentary Group.


Affiliations
Member of the Mariagerfjord Town Council 2001-2006, member of the technical and environmemtal committee. Member of the Municipal Council in Mariagerfjord Municipality 2006-2011, member of the amalgamation committee 2006, member of the technical and environmemtal committee 2007-2011. Member of the Tax Board of Appeal in Randers-Mariagerfjord 2005-2011. Chairman of the Danish Road Safety Council from 2016.
