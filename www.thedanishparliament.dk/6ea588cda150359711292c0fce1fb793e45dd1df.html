Erling Bonnesen, born March 28th 1955.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Funen greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Funen County constituency from October 7th 2004 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Svendborg nomination district from 2005.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Middelfart nomination district from 2001 to 2005.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on environment, food, agriculture and animal welfare. Clerk of Parliament from 2015. Secretary of the parliamentary group of the Libaral Party from 2015.


Education and jobs
Financial consultant and State-authorised Public Accountant's examination, from 1980 to 1989.
Military service, the Royal Life Guards, from 1978 to 1979.
Technician on the finance line with sojourns in Malmö and Oslo, from 1974 to 1978.

State-authorised public accountant, from 1990.


Affiliations
Work for associations and boards, the local community, sports and athletics, constituency organisations, etc. from 1980. Mayor of Broby Municipality 1994-2006. Member of the governing body of the association of local authorities 'Det Skæve Danmark' 1998-2002. Member of the governing body of the National Association of Local Authorities 2002-2004. Vice-chairman of the association of local authorities on the island of Funen 2002-2004.
