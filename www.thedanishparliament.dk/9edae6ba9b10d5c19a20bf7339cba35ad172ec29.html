Peter Juel Jensen, born May 18th 1966 in Rønne, son of former business manager Jens Juel Jensen and former mayoress Birthe Juel Jensen. Has the children Jeppe, born in 2002, Rasmus, born in 2004, and Asta-Maria, born in 2006.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Bornholm greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Rønne nomination district from 2005.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Aakirkeby nomination district from 2005.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on defence. Chairman of the Rural Districts and Islands Committee from 2015 and of the Scrutineers’ Committee  from 2011, vice-chairman 2009-2011. Member of  the Defence Committee, the Foreign Affairs Committee, the Employment Committee, the Children’s and Education Committee, the Greenland Committee, the Gender Equality Committee and the Research, Innovation and Further Education Committee. Chairman of the Danish delegation at the OSCE Parliamentary Assembly from 2008.

 

 

 

 


Education and jobs
Teacher education, Hjørring College of Education, from 1996 to 2000.
Officer of the line, Frederiksberg Castle, from 1991 to 1993.
Basic vocational education (EFG) and higher commercial examination (HH), Bornholm Vocational School, Rønne, from 1984 to 1987.

Deputy head teacher, Østermarie and Østerlars/Gudhjem Schools, from 2006 to 2007.
Teacher, Rønne Municipal School Authority, from 2000 to 2006.
First lieutenant, the Household Guards, Næstved, from 1993 to 1996.


Affiliations
Chairman of Principal Organisation of Officers at Frederiksberg Castle (HOC) 1992-1993. Chairman of the Student Council at Hjørring College of Education 1996-2000. Member of Bornholm County Council 2001-2002. Member of Aakirkeby Municipal Council 2001-2002, member of the technology and environment committee and the finance committee. Chairman of Bornholm Academy 2001-2007. Chairman of the education council at Åvang School in Rønne 2002-2004. Member of the Regional Council in Bornholm Regional Municipality 2002-2007, member of the Amalgamation Committee, the Technology and Environment Committee, the Business and Labour Market Committee and the Employment Committee. Member of the management committee of Rønne Harbour 2005-2007. Member of the management committee of ‘NaturBornholm’ (Nature Bornholm) 2005-2007. Member of the board of 'Folk og Sikkerhed', Bornholm from 2006.
