Søren Egge Rasmussen, born July 2nd 1961 in Fussingø Vandmølle, son of forest manager Roald Rasmussen and laboratory technician Ingrid Rasmussen. Partner Ina Marie Graneberg.


Member period
Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in East Jutland greater constituency from June 18th 2015.
Temporary Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Århus County constituency from March 21st 2006 to April 18th 2006.
Temporary Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Århus County constituency from December 12th 2005 to December 18th 2005.
Temporary Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Århus County constituency from May 23rd 2002 to May 30th 2002.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Århus North nomination district from 2001.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on energy, food, fisheries, telecommununication and housing.


Education and jobs
Farm management training (green certificate), Den Økologiske Landbrugsskole (school of organic agriculture) in Kalø, 2005.
Organic farmer, Den Økologiske Landbrugsskole (school of organic agriculture) in Store Vildmose, from 1999 to 2002.
Carpenter , Frode Nielsens Tømrer og Snedkerforretning (carpenters and cabinetmakers), from 1987 to 1990.
Upper Secondary School certificate, Randers Statsskole, from 1979 to 1981.

Secretary, The Red-Green Alliance, from 2009 to 2010.
Farmer, from 1999 to 2010.
Carpenter, from 1987 to 1999.


Affiliations
Member of Aarhus City Council for the Red-Green Alliance, 1994-2001. Member of the Technology Committee 1994-2001, Chairman 1998-2001, the Committee for Real Estate and Green Zones 1994-1997 and member of the Committee for Municipal Enterprises 1998-2001. Member of the Board of NRGi (energy company), 2000-2015, Chairman, 2010-2015. Member of the Board of Representatives of NRGi (energy company) since 2000. Member of the Board of Gudenåcentralen (CHP plant), 2002-2006. Deputy Chairman of Midtjyske Net (grid owner), 2006-2012. Member of the Board of N1, 2006-2012. Member of the Board of Lejerbo Aarhus (housing association) since 2007. Member of the Board of EL:CON (electrical services company), 2008-2015. Member of the Board of Representatives of Merkur since 2009. Chairman of the Board of Havvind Aarhus Bugt (wind energy supply company), 2010-2015.
