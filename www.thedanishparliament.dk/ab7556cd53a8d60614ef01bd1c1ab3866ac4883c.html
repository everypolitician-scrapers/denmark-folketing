Søren Pind, born November 20th 1969 in Herning, son of dentist Torben Pind and physiotherapist Jytte Pind.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Copenhagen greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Eastern Copenhagen constituency from February 8th 2005 to November 13th 2007.
Temporary Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Bornholm County constituency from May 26th 1997 to June 1st 1997.
Temporary Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Bornholm County constituency from December 13th 1994 to December 19th 1994.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Østerbro nomination district from 2002.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Rønne nomination district from 1991 to 1998.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Aakirkeby nomination district from 1991 to 1998.

Parliamentary career
Minister for Higher Education and Science from November 28th 2016.
Minister for Justice from June 28th 2015 to November 28th 2016.
Minister for Refugee, Immigration and Integration Affairs and Minister for Development Cooperation from March 8th 2011 to October 3rd 2011.
Minister for Development Cooperation from February 23rd 2010 to March 8th 2011.

Education and jobs
Master of Laws, University of Copenhagen, 1997.
Matriculated, Bornholm County Upper Secondary School, 1988.

Co-owner, Propeople/FFW, from 2008.
External lecturer, constitutional law, University of Copenhagen, from 2008 to 2010.
Contact Manager, Prepeople, from 2008 to 2010.
Tutor in constitutional law, University of Copenhagen, from 1999 to 2009.
Project manager, Waterfront Communication, from 1997 to 1998.


Affiliations
Member of the national management committee of the Young Liberals 1986-1988 and 1989-1991, member of the executive committee 1989-1991. Member of Copenhagen City Council 1994-2008, mayor with responsibility for building and technology administration 1998-2006. Chairman of Copenhagen Housing Commission 1998-2006. Chairman of 'Vejnavnenævnet' (The Board of Road Names) and of the 'Det Grønne Råd' (The Green Council) 1998-2006. Member of 'Det Særlige Bygningssyn' (The Historic Buildings Council) 1999-2003. Member of the Greater Copenhagen Development Council (HUR) 2000-2002. Consul general for 'KulturBornholm' 2002. Member of the board of directors of 'Københavns Havn A/S' (Port of Copenhagen), 'Prøvestenen' development and the 'Vestskov' Committee 2002-2006. Vice-chairman of 'Københavns Energi A/S' (Copenhagen Energy) 2006-2007. Member of the board of Nordicom 2008-2010.


Publications
Published »Friheden duer til alt godt« (Freedom is Good for Everything Good), 2002, »Kære Søren - en brevveksling om det nye Danmarks kurs« (Dear Søren, an Exchange of Letters on the Course of the New Denmark) with Søren Krarup, 2003, »Lise Nørgaard - et portræt« (Lise Nørgaard – a Portrait), 2005, and »Fra i dag - Aznavour på dansk« (From today - Aznavour in Danish) CD with Pelle Voigt, 2004.
