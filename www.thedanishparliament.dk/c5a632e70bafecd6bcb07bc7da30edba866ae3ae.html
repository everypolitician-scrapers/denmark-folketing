Marie Krarup, born December 6th 1965 at Seem near Ribe, daughter of incumbent Søren Krarup and housewife Elisabeth Krarup. Has two children.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in South Jutland greater constituency from September 15th 2011.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Varde nomination district from 2013.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Greater Esbjerg nomination district from 2010 to 2013.

Parliamentary career

Spokeswoman on defence, emergency preparedness from 2011 and upper secondary school from 2012.


Education and jobs
Post-graduate teacher training, Odsherred Upper Secondary School, from 2006 to 2007.
Subsidiary subject in religion, Open University, University of Copenhagen, 2006.
BCom 1 and subjects in BCom 2, Copenhagen Business School, 2003.
MA (political science in the Eastern States and social studies), University of Copenhagen and Aarhus University, 1996.
Officer of the Reserve, basic training in Russian, Svanemøllens Barracks, from 1985 to 1987.
Theology studies, from 1984 to 1985.
Matriculation certificate in the classical languages line, Ribe Cathedral School, 1984.

Senior teacher, Frederiksborg Upper Secondary School, from 2007 to 2011.
Analyst, expert on Eastern Europe and the SNG, 'Eksportkreditfonden', from 2003 to 2005.
Market analyst, House of Prince, from 2001 to 2002.
Assistant defence attaché, Major, Danish Embassy in Moscow, from 1998 to 2001.
Head of section, International Division of the Danish Defence, from 1997 to 1998.
Teacher, VUC Falster, Greve Upper Secondary School and Danish Defence Special School, from 1996 to 1997.
Officer of the Reserve, The Danish Defence, from 1987 to 1997.
