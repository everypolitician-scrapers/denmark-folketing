Morten Østergaard, born June 17th 1976 in Århus, son of chief physician in cardiology and doctor of medical sciences Bent Østergaard Kristensen MD and senior lecturer Ingrid Bayer Kristensen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in East Jutland greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in Århus County constituency from February 8th 2005 to November 13th 2007.
Temporary Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in Århus County constituency from January 26th 2004 to February 1st 2004.
Temporary Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in Århus County constituency from January 25th 2003 to January 31st 2003.
Temporary Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in Århus County constituency from April 20th 2002 to April 26th 2002.
Temporary Member of Parliament for the Social Liberal Party in Århus County constituency from January 21st 2002 to January 27th 2002.
Candidate for the Social Liberal Party in Århus East nomination district from 2007.
Candidate for the Social Liberal Party in Århus West nomination district from 1999 to 2006.

Parliamentary career
Minister for Economic Affairs and the Interior from September 2nd 2014 to June 28th 2015.
Minister for Taxation from February 3rd 2014 to September 2nd 2014.
Minister for Science, Innovation and Higher Education from October 3rd 2011 to February 3rd 2014.

Chairman of the parliamentary group of the Social Liberal Party from 2015, vice-chairman 2007-2011, member of the management group from 2006. Political leader of the Social Liberal Party from 2014, vice-chairman of the Social Liberal Party 2002-2005.


Education and jobs
MSc (Political Science), Aarhus University, from 1997 to 2006.
Risskov County Upper Secondary School, from 1993 to 1996.
Conval High School, USA, from 1992 to 1993.
Jellebakke School, Risskov, from 1981 to 1992.

Market manager, Dafolo A/S, Aarhus, from 2001 to 2005.


Affiliations
Member of the council of representatives of the National Bank from 2015.


Publications
Author of »Digital Forkalkning« (Digital Calcification), Dafolo, 2004.
