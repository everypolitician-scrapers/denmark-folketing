Rune Lund, born October 30th 1976 in Copenhagen, son of journalist Kurt Lund and senior lecturer Gunhild Paaske. Married. Has three children.


Member period
Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Funen greater constituency from June 18th 2015.
Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Funen County constituency from February 8th 2005 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Middelfart nomination district from 2014.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Odense East nomination district from 2003 to 2008.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Otterup nomination district from 2003 to 2008.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on constitutional matters, legal affairs and fiscal affairs. Former spokesman on municipal affairs, IT, foreign policy, defence, the EU and transport. Chairman of the parliamentary group of the Unity List 2006-2007


Education and jobs
MSc (political science), University of Copenhagen, from 1998 to 2007.
Matriculated from Vestre Borgerdyd Upper Secondary School, from 1992 to 1995.
Primary school, Valby School and Mariendal Free School, from 1983 to 1992.

Economic consultant, Gladsaxe Municipality, Social and Health Services Department, from 2013 to 2015.
Economic consultant, New York, USA, from 2011 to 2013.
Economic consultant, Gladsaxe Municipality, Social and Health Services Department, from 2009 to 2011.
Self-employed, entrepreneur, Copenhagen, from 2007 to 2008.
Coordinator and head of secretariat, NGO Forum - Stop Volden, from 2002 to 2003.
Assistant educator, Vangedehuse, from 1998 to 2001.
Home care assistant, Holte, from 1996 to 1997.


Affiliations
Chairman of the Student Council at Vestre Borgerdyd Gymnasium, 1994-1995, Chairman of "Operation Dagsværk" group (student charity organisation). Member of the Board of the Danish Upper Secondary School Students Union (DGS), 1994-1995. National Chairman of "Operation Dagsværk" (national student charity organisation) Member of The Red-Green Alliance since 1996. Peace worker in Guatamala for Internationalt Forum (solidarity organization), 1997-1998 Volunteer worker for the Central America committee – working in slum districts of Nicaragua to identify potential aid projects, 1998. Active in "Initiativet for et andet Europa", the Danish left-wing's principal organisation for demonstrations and counter-summits in connection with EU summit meetings, 1999-2001. Member of the editorial staff of SALT (cultural journal), 2001-2003. Member of the Board of the Red-Green Alliance, 2002-2006, 2008-2011 and 2014-2015, member of the Executive Committee, 2002-2005.
