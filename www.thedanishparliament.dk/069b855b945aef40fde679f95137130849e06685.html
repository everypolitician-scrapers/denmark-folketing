Hans Christian Schmidt, born August 25th 1953, son of Ingrid A. Schmidt and Holger Schmidt.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in South Jutland greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in South Jutland County constituency from September 21st 1994 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Haderslev nomination district from 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Rødding nomination district from 1994 to 2006.

Parliamentary career
Minister for Transportation and Buildings from June 28th 2015 to November 28th 2016.
Minister for Transport from February 23rd 2010 to October 3rd 2011.
Minister for Food, Agriculture and Fisheries from August 2nd 2004 to September 12th 2007.
Minister for the Environment from November 27th 2001 to August 2nd 2004.

Chairman of the Rural Districts and Islands Committee 2011-2015. Former member of the Transport Committee, the Health Committee, the Business, Growth and Export Committee, the Food, Agricultural and Fisheries Committee, the Climate, Energy and Buildings Committee, the Environment Committee and the Ecclesiastical Affairs Committee. Former member of the Board of the Danish Inter-Parliamentary Group. Environment policy spokesman 1995-2001. Group chairman of the Liberal Party 2007-2010.


Education and jobs
Teachers' certificate examination, Haderslev Teacher Training College, 1977.
Higher preparatory examination, Kolding Upper Secondary School, 1973.
Commercial examination, Haderslev Business College, 1971.
Lower secondary school examination, Nustrup School, 1970.

Deputy head teacher, from 1984 to 1994.
Teacher, from 1977 to 1984.
Teacher, Vojens Musikskole, from 1975 to 1994.


Affiliations
Member of Vojens Municipal Council 1982-2001, chairman of the education and social services committee 1990-1994 and 1998-2001, first Deputy Mayor 1998-2001. Member of the Nature Protection Board of Appeal 1995-2001. Chairman of Vojens 'Kultur- og Musikhus' 2000-2001.
