Aaja Chemnitz Arnatsiaq Larsen, born December 2nd 1977 in Nuuk, daughter of Jørgen Schmidt Chemnitz and Jette Larsen. Lives with Michael Driefer.


Member period
Member of Parliament for Inuit Ataqatigiit in Greenland from June 18th 2015.
Candidate for Inuit Ataqatigiit in Greenland nomination district from 2014.

Parliamentary career

Member of the Landsting (Parliament) of Greenland since 2014.


Education and jobs
Executive Management, INSEAD, 2011.
M.Sc. Administration, Ilisimatusarfik (University of Greenland), Ilisimatusarfik, from 1998 to 2004.

Children spokeswoman, Parliament of Greenland, from 2012 to 2015.
Director, Welfare administration, Kommuneqarfik Sermerssooq, from 2009 to 2012.
Head of Department, Nuuk Municipality, from 2006 to 2008.
Associate Expert, United Nations, New York, from 2006 to 2007.
Head of section, The Premier's Office, Greenland Home Rule Government, from 2004 to 2006.


Affiliations
Chairman of the Board of Bedre Børneliv (Better Life for Children) 2008-2009 and 2012. Member of the Board of Great Greenland 2011-2013. Deputy Chairman and member of the Board of Transparency International Greenland 2012-2014. Member of the Board of Statistics Greenland 2012-2014. Member of the Board of the Danish Institute for Human Rights 2012-2014. Deputy Chairman of the National Council of Human Rights in Greenland 2013, Chairman 2014. Chairman of the National Council for Children 2012-2015. Chairman of the Board of Tele-Post 2012-2015. Board member of IKIU (independent legal aid institution) from 2015.


Awards
Leader of Årets Arbejdsplads (Danish trade union 3F's workplace of the year award), 2007. Save the Children Denmark's Children's Rights Award 2013.
