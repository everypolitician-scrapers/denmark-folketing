Jan Johansen, born December 12th 1955 in Svendborg, son of semi-skilled worker Preben Johansen and housewife Gerda Johansen. Married to Jette Hye Johansen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in Funen greater constituency from September 15th 2011.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Nyborg nomination district from 2010.

Education and jobs
Vice-chairman, the United Federation of Danish Workers (3F) East Funen, from 2005 to 2011.
Chairman, the General Union of Workers (SiD) Munkebo, from 2001 to 2005.
Semi-skilled worker, 'Lindøværftet', from 1974 to 2001.
Semi-skilled worker, 'Bullerup Møbelfabrik', from 1973 to 1974.
Sailor, from 1970 to 1972.


Affiliations
Shop steward for service workers at 'Lindøværftet' 1989-2001. Employee representative on the board of Lindøværftet A/S 1992-2001. Member of the governing body and later chairman of the Youth School in Munkebo Municipality 1996-2007. Member of the town council in Munkebo Municipality 1997-2006, deputy mayor and chairman of the Social Services Committee 2001-2006. Chairman of the Danish Federation of Trade Unions (LO) Munkebo 2001-2005. Member of the governing body and later vice-chairman of Kerteminde Production High School 2002-2010. Chairman of the Municipal Policy Committee, Kerteminde, LO East Funen from 2005. Chairman of the Local Employment Council 2006-2009. Member of the town council in Kerteminde Municipality 2006-2011, member of the amalgamation committee 2006, chairman of the Committee for the Elderly and Disabled.
